Foam FAQ

Everything before the foam flies

The questions we get on nearly every call — answered up front so booking is easy.

Yes. Our foam solution is non-toxic, tear-free, and hypoallergenic. It's specifically formulated for parties and is safe for kids, pets, and sensitive skin. As with any soap-based product, we recommend rinsing off afterward and keeping foam out of eyes just to be comfortable.
Completely biodegradable. The foam breaks down naturally within a couple of hours after we finish, and it won't harm your grass, garden beds, or landscaping.
We recommend a flat, grassy area at least 20×20 ft, though we can work with slightly smaller spaces — just let us know your yard's dimensions when you book so we can plan the setup.
Just a standard outdoor electrical outlet within reach of an extension cord, and access to a garden hose for water. We bring the machine, solution, and everything else needed to get set up.
If weather forces a cancellation, we'll work with you to find a new date that works. We keep an eye on the forecast leading up to your event and will reach out to coordinate if rain looks likely.
Swimsuits or clothes you don't mind getting wet and a little sudsy. We suggest towels and a change of clothes for afterward, and water shoes or sandals if the ground gets slippery.
Foam parties are best enjoyed on warm days — we generally recommend outdoor temperatures of 70°F or higher for guest comfort. If your event date looks unseasonably cool, reach out and we'll help you figure out the best plan.
Very little for you to do. The foam dissipates naturally within a couple of hours, and we handle breakdown of our equipment. A quick hose-down of any hard surfaces (patios, driveways) afterward is all we'd suggest.
Weekends fill up fastest, especially June through August, so we recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks ahead. That said, give us a call — we'll always try to make a last-minute date work if we're free.
